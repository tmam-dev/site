Faq

What's AI red teaming ?

AI red teaming involves simulating adversarial attacks on AI systems to identify vulnerabilities, biases, and potential risks before they are exploited in real-world scenarios.

What is LLM Observerability?

LLM (Large Language Model) observability and monitoring refer to the practices and tools used to track, analyze, and manage the behavior of large language models in real time.

Where can I test different AI models?

You can use Pixa's AI Playground to test different models, including GPT4, Claude, Perplexity and more.
